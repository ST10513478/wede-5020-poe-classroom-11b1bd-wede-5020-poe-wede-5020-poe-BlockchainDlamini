History

A small business established in 2022 by a young South African man named lucky, who started off operating from the backroom of his father’s house to owning his first retail space. With the goal and vision to provide tech solutions to members in his community, from high school learners to college students, working professionals and those who just want their personal devices functioning as good as new at an affordable price without having to go the distance.
